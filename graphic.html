Brief & Mission

The Project

The project brief was to design an app that promotes meditation and to define its visual branding strategy.

  1. Design and develop the logo to reflect the company's core values.
  2. Create the color palette, typography, and icon set.
  3. Design the graphic asset tailored for social media communication.

Logo and concept

I designed the logo

The logo intertwines visual elements that symbolize the core principles of meditation, centrality, and awareness, in harmony with nature and the sunrise. The eye represents perception and introspection, while the sun evokes renewal, positive energy, and the cycle of nature, bringing warmth and vitality.

Together, these elements convey a sense of balance, offering a visual metaphor for the "active calm" central to MeditActive's identity: a space to pause, reconnect, and find harmony.

Logo variations

Logo and concept

The logo structure

The logo is built on a harmonious geometric construction: circles and guidelines define a balanced shape that combines the eye with the rising sun. A simple yet meaningful structure, conveying calm, centrality, and a connection with nature.

Geometric construction

Guidelines and harmonic circles define the shape of the logo, ensuring balance and visual consistency.

Clear space and proportions

The protective margin ensures readability and recognizability of the logo in any context of use.

Logo and concept

Color palette

The palette evokes calm, balance, and a bond with nature.

Blues suggest harmony and gentle movement, cream white adds lightness and mental openness, and greens convey sustainability, stability, and grounding.

Together, they create a sense of "active calm," a safe space to pause and reset.

Logo and concept

Icon set concept

I created six icons aligned with MeditActive's logo, palette, and values. They balance calm and mindfulness—through symbols like the lotus—with movement and vitality inspired by yoga, using brand colors to reinforce harmony and energy.

Social media communication

Social media

For the brand's social media, I chose images to engage young people (15–40) interested in meditation. They convey peace and joy in simple moments—like meditating under a tree—focusing on emotional impact rather than app usage, and inviting viewers to pause and disconnect. The style is vintage and natural, with soft sunrise-inspired colors, while the tone is colloquial and direct, capturing attention and reinforcing calm and mindfulness.
